What is resin

Resin Epoxy is a chemical liquid can be decorated and colored as required, then poured into desired molds that shape the item , takes up to 10 days for the initial hardener and up to 30 days for full hardned, depends on the weather.
It has a shiny and very clear look like glass, but the final touch will feel like plastic.
can be affected by fire and heat.
